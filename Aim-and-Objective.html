AIM & OBJECTIVES

AIM & OBJECTIV​​E

                                                                                                     

The aim of Cookridge Academy School
To provide a friendly, clean, comfortable and safe environment for children which will be
stimulating and challenging, and where enjoyment, fun and achievement are linked with discovery and learning. 
To meet the above Aims we are committed to:
Delivering a high standard of service to develop each child in our care.
Respecting each child as an individual taking in to account their own needs.
Ensuring that our staff are recruited, trained and supervised to meet the needs of the children in their care.
Providing a safe, healthy and friendly environment for the children to play and learn.
Ensuring that our service is ran in accordance with the latest standards of care.
Valuing partnerships with parents/ guardians/ carers and other outside agencies
Our Aims for Children:
-We aim to ensure that each child:
-Is in a safe and stimulating environment
-Is given generous care and attention.
-Has a personal key person to ensure that each child makes satisfying progress;
-Is in a setting that sees parents as partners in helping each child to learn and develop and parents help to maintain the services that it offers.
-At Cookridge Academy School, we aim to follow the guidance and adhere to the standards to ensure that children thrive and are safe -whilst they play in our pre-school. ·
-To ensure children have access to quality teaching of broad and enriched curriculum within the community. ·
-To engender a positive attitude to learning and celebrate success
The objective of Cookridge Academy School
Is to provide a high standard of care and education for all the children in our care:
Ensuring smooth physical, mental and emotionally development in children and acquisition of positive habits
Preparing children for primary education.
