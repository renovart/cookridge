MISSION

Based on four principal themes; a unique Child, Positive Relationships, Enabling Environments, Learning and Development and ensuring that children are safe and well looked after and, most importantly, having fun. It covers two key areas; welfare and the development of Cookridge Academy School will provide care and education for children aged 2 years to 5 years old.  Our professional staff will provide a safe environment where learning and play come together, ensuring your child is happy and protected at all times. Our vision is to provide a good quality service to the community it serves, supporting children’s individual needs and allowing growth within a safe and happy environment. Our provision will follow the Early Learning Foundation Stage children.
The guidance divides children’s learning and development into a) Characteristics of Effective Learning, b) three prime areas and C ) four specific areas: a)  Characteristics of Effective Learning

For each area the guidance sets milestones and from these milestones we can work out where a child is and then plan activities to help children develop for the next steps. It also helps us to identify if a child needs extra support. The guidance also sets out stepping stones for each area of learning which describe the stages through which children are likely to pass as they move towards achievement of the goal. These stages overlap each other as children may develop more strongly in one area, whilst needing support in another. We will spend time observing each child and playing with them, and from this we plan what we do next. We create activities that will help children develop in a fun way and also support them in gaining confidence whilst developing a new skill.
